About Us

Who We are

As one of Turkey's leading hardware companies, Mete Ticaret has joined forces with Pars, the country’s top manufacturer of safety footwear and tactical boots. By combining our 30 years of export experience with Pars’ 20 years of production expertise, we have proudly entered the American and European markets through this partnership.

With significant investments in R&D, Pars operates two state-of-the-art factories dedicated to producing high-quality safety footwear and tactical boots. This collaboration enables us to deliver innovative, durable, and comfortable products that meet international safety standards, ensuring the best protection for workers worldwide.
